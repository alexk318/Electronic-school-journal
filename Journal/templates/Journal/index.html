Electronic school Journal

Digitalization is an important step in the twenty-first century

What?

“Electronic diary and journal” - a service that allows participants in the educational process to receive information about training schedules, current and final grades and homework online. Also, electronic school workflow should ensure a reduction in the administrative burden on educational institutions. In addition, various systems of electronic diaries can support other, additional functions - electronic student portfolios, news feeds, social networks and so on.

Why?

  • Less paperwork
  • Honest evaluation
  • You will never lose him

What for?

  • Teacher curriculum development
  • Compilation of statistics on attendance / academic performance of students
  • Additional remote tasks
